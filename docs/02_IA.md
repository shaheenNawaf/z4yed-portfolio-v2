# Purpose

This document defines the complete information architecture of the portfolio.

Its goal is to ensure that every section appears in the optimal order to maximize recruiter comprehension, reduce cognitive load, and increase interview conversions.

This document defines WHAT exists.

It does not define HOW it looks.

---

# Core Principle

The homepage should tell a story.

Not present a resume.

Every section exists because it answers a specific hiring question.

Every section should naturally lead into the next.

# Reading Pattern

Design around the F-pattern.

Users should understand every section through scanning.

Large paragraphs are prohibited.

Large screenshots are encouraged.

Cards should summarize.

Expansion reveals detail.

---

# Navigation Strategy

Navbar should not appear immediately.

The Hero owns the user's attention.

# Information Priority

Tier 1

Must always be visible.

- Name
- Positioning
- CTA

Tier 2

Immediately visible.

- Metrics
- Featured Projects

Tier 3

Expandable.

- Timeline
- Experience
- Technical Details

Tier 4

Hidden until requested.

- Long-form case studies
- Architecture
- Technical writeups

---

# Non-Goals

Do not include

- Generic About Me
- Long personal biography
- Skill icon walls
- Decorative statistics
- Generic testimonials
- Blog posts
- Social feed
- Endless scrolling galleries

Every section must justify its existence.
