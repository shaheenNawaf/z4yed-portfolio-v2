# Hero Section Specification

Version: 1.0
Status: Draft

---

# Purpose

The Hero section is the single most important section on the website.

Its purpose is to immediately communicate my positioning, differentiate me from traditional software engineers and marketers, and convince recruiters to continue scrolling.

A visitor should understand my value proposition within 5 seconds.

---

# Recruiter Question

Who is this person and why should I keep reading?

---

# Primary Objective

Increase scroll depth.

---

# Secondary Objectives

Increase resume downloads.

Increase LinkedIn clicks.

Increase project exploration.

Increase recruiter confidence.

---

# Key Message

I am a Growth Marketer with Software Engineering depth.

I don't just market products.

I build products that convert.

---

# Information Hierarchy

The Hero must communicate information in this exact order.

1. Name

2. Position

3. Value Proposition

4. Primary CTA

5. Secondary CTA

6. Supporting Metrics

7. Professional Portrait

Nothing else.

---

# Layout

Desktop

Two-column layout.

Left

- Headline
- Supporting Copy
- CTA Buttons
- Quick Metrics

Right

- Portrait
- Subtle animated background
- Floating technology indicators (optional)

Desktop ratio

55 / 45

---

Tablet

Portrait moves below headline.

Buttons remain side-by-side.

Metrics wrap naturally.

---

Mobile

Portrait first.

Headline.

Supporting copy.

CTA.

Metrics.

Everything vertically stacked.

---

# Headline

The headline should be large.

Maximum two lines.

Examples

"Growth Marketer × Software Engineer"

or

"I Build Products That Convert."

Avoid buzzwords.

Avoid clever marketing copy.

Clarity wins.

---

# Supporting Copy

Maximum three lines.

Communicate

- Growth mindset
- Engineering execution
- Business impact

Avoid life story.

Avoid mission statements.

---

# CTA

Primary

View Selected Work

Secondary

Download Resume

Never add more than two CTAs.

---

# Metrics

Maximum four.

Examples

4+ Years Experience

$355K+ Campaign Volume

22K+ Community Members

25+ SEA Creator Partnerships

Metrics should appear as compact cards.

Never dominate the Hero.

---

# Portrait

Professional.

High contrast.

No busy background.

Rounded corners.

Should visually balance the text.

---

# Navigation

Navbar must NOT be visible while the Hero is fully in view.

The Hero owns the user's attention.

Navbar appears only after the Hero scrolls out of view.

---

# Motion

Headline

Fade + slight upward motion.

Portrait

Subtle fade.

Buttons

Fade.

Metrics

Staggered reveal.

Avoid excessive animation.

---

# Accessibility

Proper heading hierarchy.

Keyboard accessible buttons.

Descriptive alt text.

Readable contrast.

---

# Performance

Portrait optimized.

Lazy load decorative assets.

Avoid blocking animations.

---

# Success Criteria

A recruiter should understand within five seconds:

✓ Who I am.

✓ What I do.

✓ Why I am different.

✓ Where to click next.

---

# Common Mistakes

Do NOT

- Add rotating text.
- Add typing animations.
- Add carousels.
- Add more than two buttons.
- Add walls of text.
- Add decorative statistics.
- Add social media icons.
- Add technology logos.
- Add unnecessary badges.
