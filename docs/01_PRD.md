# 1. Overview

This portfolio is not a digital resume.

It is a conversion-focused product whose primary purpose is to convince recruiters, hiring managers, startup founders, and headhunters that I am a rare hybrid professional capable of delivering measurable business outcomes through both Growth Marketing and Software Engineering.

The portfolio should communicate competence within seconds, establish credibility through evidence instead of claims, and progressively reveal deeper information as visitors continue exploring.

This portfolio should feel closer to a premium SaaS product than a personal website.

---

# 2. Vision

Create the highest-converting recruiter portfolio possible.

Every interaction, component, animation, piece of copy, and section should reduce hiring friction and increase confidence that I can build products with measurable business impact.

The portfolio should not attempt to impress through excessive visuals.

Instead, it should impress through clarity, execution quality, and demonstrated outcomes.

# 13. Design Principles

Every section must answer exactly one hiring question.

Every claim must be supported by proof.

Every screen should have one primary action.

Large visuals are preferred over large paragraphs.

Reading effort should be minimized.

Progressive disclosure should be preferred over information overload.

Whitespace should create focus rather than emptiness.

Motion should guide attention, never distract.

---

# 14. Non Goals

This portfolio is NOT intended to be:

- A blog
- A design portfolio
- An agency landing page
- A freelancer marketplace profile
- A social media feed
- A documentation website
